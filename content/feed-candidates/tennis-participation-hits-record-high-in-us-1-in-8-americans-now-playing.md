Tennis participation in the United States reached its highest proportion on record in 2025, with 1 in 8 Americans (ages 6+) playing tennis at least once. The biggest growth came from Black/African Americans (+14% growth, +454K participants) and seniors aged 65+ (+14% growth, +299K participants). This surge reflects tennis's expanding appeal across diverse demographics and age groups.
