STRI, the research group that monitors surface performance for the LTA and the All England Club, flagged that an unusually warm start to the 2026 summer is one of the key variables affecting grass court conditions across the season. Qualifying players were already dealing with heat expected to peak at 93°F during the pre-tournament rounds. Grass courts are uniquely sensitive to heat stress compared to hard or clay surfaces — worn turf cannot be resurfaced mid-tournament, meaning early-round conditions directly shape how the court plays by the second week.
