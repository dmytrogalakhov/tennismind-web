The All England Club raised its 2026 prize pot by £10.7 million to £64.2 million — a 20% increase described by player representatives as 'the largest single-year uplift in the tournament's history.' Yet leading players from both tours have confirmed they will continue prize money protests at the event, limiting media duties despite welcoming the announcement. The gap between record generosity and ongoing protest reveals the core dispute isn't about the absolute number but about structural revenue share — players have been pushing Grand Slams on that issue for over a year.
