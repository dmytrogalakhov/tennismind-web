The majority of tennis racket manufacturing now takes place in Taiwan and China due to their ability to produce high-quality rackets at significantly lower costs than traditional manufacturing locations. These factories use advanced injection molding machines and aerospace-derived materials, representing a major shift from the sport's traditional equipment manufacturing centers in Europe and North America.
