Hugo Boss became title sponsor of the Stuttgart ATP tournament in 2022, and after just four years has already extended the deal through 2030. The fashion company's commitment reflects the tournament's success in blending tennis with lifestyle branding, featuring BOSS-dressed ball kids, branded entertainment areas, and fashion experiences alongside world-class tennis.
