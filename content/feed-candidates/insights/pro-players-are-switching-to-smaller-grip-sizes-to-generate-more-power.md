Jack Draper recently switched from grip size 4 to grip size 3, noting that modern players are using smaller grips compared to 10-20 years ago when bigger grips and heavier rackets were the norm. The smaller grip allows players to generate more racket head speed and whip through the ball, helping with power shots and spin generation like Draper's slider serve.
