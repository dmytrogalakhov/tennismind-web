Tatjana Maria, who won Queen's Club in 2025, was denied a wildcard for the 2026 tournament and called for 'respect' from organizers. This highlights the unusual position defending champions can find themselves in when their ranking drops - Maria's world ranking apparently fell enough that she needed wildcard entry. It's rare for a defending champion at a WTA 500 event to be refused entry to defend their title.
