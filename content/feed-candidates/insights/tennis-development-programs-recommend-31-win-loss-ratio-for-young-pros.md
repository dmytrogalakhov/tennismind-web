The Ontario Tennis Association's Long Term Athlete Development model recommends that players aged 17-23 maintain a 3:1 win-loss ratio as they transition to professional tennis. This systematic approach prioritizes long-term development over short-term results, suggesting that even future pros should lose 25% of their matches during crucial developmental years to ensure proper growth.
