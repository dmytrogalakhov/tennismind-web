Rafael Nadal holds the record for the longest unbeaten run at Roland Garros with 39 consecutive wins between 2010 and 2015. During this period, he also won 5 consecutive titles from 2010 to 2014. The next-best winning streak belongs to Björn Borg with 28 matches, showing just how unprecedented Nadal's clay court dominance has been.
