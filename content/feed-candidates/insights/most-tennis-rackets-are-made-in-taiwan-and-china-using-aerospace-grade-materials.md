Modern tennis rackets are manufactured using materials borrowed from aerospace technology, including carbon fiber and specialized resins that create lightweight frames with exceptional strength. Most production happens in Taiwan and China due to their ability to produce high-quality rackets at low cost using advanced injection molding machines. This explains why even premium racket brands can offer cutting-edge composite technology at accessible price points.
