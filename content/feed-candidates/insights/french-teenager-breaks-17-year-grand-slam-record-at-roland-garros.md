17-year-old Moise Kouame became the youngest man to win a Grand Slam match in 17 years when he defeated former US Open champion Marin Cilic 7-6(4) 6-2 6-1 at Roland Garros 2026. He's also the youngest player to advance past the first round at Roland Garros since Romania's Dinu Pescariu in 1991. This breakthrough highlights how Roland Garros continues to be the launching pad for tennis's next generation, just as it was for teenage champions like Borg, Chang, and Seles.
