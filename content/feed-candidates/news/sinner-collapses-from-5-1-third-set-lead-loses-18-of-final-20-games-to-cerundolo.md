World No. 1 Jannik Sinner was up two sets and 5-1 in the third when he 'hit a wall' and lost in five sets to Juan Manuel Cerundolo. Andre Agassi criticized Sinner courtside, saying 'there's no excuse for him to run into a wall at 1:45.'
