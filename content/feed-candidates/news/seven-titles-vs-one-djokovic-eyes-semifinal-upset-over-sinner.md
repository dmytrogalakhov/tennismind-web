Novak Djokovic carries seven Wimbledon titles into his semifinal against Jannik Sinner, the No. 1 seed and reigning champion, coming off what ESPN describes as a 'monumental win' in the quarterfinals. Sinner arrives as defending champion and tournament favourite, but Djokovic's unmatched record on the Wimbledon grass — and his form this fortnight — makes this the most compelling men's semifinal matchup of the draw. The winner faces either Zverev or Fery, with Zverev having already reached the other semifinal.
