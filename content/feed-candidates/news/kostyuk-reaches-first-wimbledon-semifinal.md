Marta Kostyuk defeated Jasmine Paolini in superb fashion to reach the Wimbledon semifinals for the first time in her career. The Ukrainian, who has become one of the most compelling stories of the grass season, now stands two wins from a Grand Slam title. It is Kostyuk's first major semifinal at Wimbledon, and she arrives there having beaten the Italian who reached the Wimbledon final just two years ago.
