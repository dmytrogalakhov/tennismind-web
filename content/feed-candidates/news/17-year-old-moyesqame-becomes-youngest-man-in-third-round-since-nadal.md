French teenager Moyesqame lit up the first week by reaching the third round at just 17 years old — the youngest man to do so at any major since Rafael Nadal in 2003. He was celebrated at a special ceremony for the opening of the fan park at Tribune Concord, where he met French fans who have adopted him as their new tennis hero.
