Jannik Sinner lost to 56th-ranked Juan Manuel Cerundolo after leading two sets to love, with his body failing to cooperate in the final three sets. With Alcaraz, Medvedev and other champions already out, 24-time Grand Slam winner Novak Djokovic is now the only man left in the draw with a previous major title. Djokovic faces Brazilian teenager João Fonseca next on Philippe Chatrier.
