2024 Paris Olympics gold medalist Qinwen Zheng lost 6-4, 6-0 to Polish qualifier Maja Chwalinska, ranked 114th and making her French Open main draw debut. The No. 5 seed was visibly emotional in her press conference, saying 'This is definitely a heavy loss' while fighting back tears. Zheng had been struggling with injury and was unable to properly prepare for the clay season.
