Novak Djokovic reached the Wimbledon semi-final and the Australian Open final in the same six-month stretch — and still called it falling short, admitting he was 'half a step late on basically any shot' against Sinner. The 39-year-old said he aims to continue playing despite the defeat ending his bid for a record 25th Grand Slam title. His assessment — 'good but not good enough' — underlines that a Wimbledon SF and AO final in one season is, by his own standard, a disappointment.
