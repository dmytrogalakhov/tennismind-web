A ballgirl collapsed from heat stroke during the Rublev-Buse first-round match as temperatures reached 90 degrees — 22 degrees above the typical May average in Paris. Multiple players, including Olympic champion Zheng Qinwen, broke down emotionally in press conferences after losing in the brutal conditions that have defined the opening days.
