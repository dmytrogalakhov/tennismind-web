Stan Wawrinka lost his final French Open match to Jesper de Jong but received a standing ovation and tribute ceremony on court. The 41-year-old three-time Grand Slam champion, who won Roland Garros in 2015, broke down saying 'I don't want to say goodbye to you here' after finishing with 46 career main draw wins at the tournament. Tournament organizers honored him with a video tribute featuring messages from Federer, Nadal, Djokovic, Alcaraz and Sinner.
