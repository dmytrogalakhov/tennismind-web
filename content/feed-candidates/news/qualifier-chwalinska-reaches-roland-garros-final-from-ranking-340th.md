24-year-old Maja Chwalinska becomes just the second woman in tournament history to reach the final as a qualifier, having been ranked 340th a month ago. She'll face 19-year-old Mirra Andreeva in Saturday's final, making it the youngest women's final in recent memory. Chwalinska's fairytale run has captivated French crowds as she went from qualifying rounds to her first Grand Slam final.
