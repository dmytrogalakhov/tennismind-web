Aryna Sabalenka opened her Wimbledon 2026 campaign with a commanding straight-sets win over qualifier Teodora Kostovic, rating her own performance an "eight out of ten" afterwards. The world No. 1 faces no such fitness drama as her male counterpart Sinner, moving through her opener with the kind of controlled aggression that has made her the tournament favourite. Sabalenka enters round two as the top seed on the women's side with her game clearly in good order on grass.
