In a wide-ranging interview published Sunday, Murray explains what tempted him into a coaching role with Jack Draper and how he is managing the psychological gap left by no longer competing. Asked directly whether he could follow Serena Williams' lead and stage a playing comeback, Murray addresses the question head-on without ruling it out. With Wimbledon less than three weeks away and Draper among the leading British contenders, Murray's courtside presence is already one of the tournament's most-watched subplots.
