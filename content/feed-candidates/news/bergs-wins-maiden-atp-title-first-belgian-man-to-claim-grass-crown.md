Zizou Bergs claimed his maiden ATP Tour title at Eastbourne on Sunday, becoming the first Belgian man ever to win a grass-court title at tour level. The Eastbourne title is an ATP 250 played the week before Wimbledon, meaning Bergs heads to SW19 on the back of a full week of competitive grass matches and a trophy. He enters the Wimbledon draw with momentum no other Belgian man has carried onto grass at this level.
