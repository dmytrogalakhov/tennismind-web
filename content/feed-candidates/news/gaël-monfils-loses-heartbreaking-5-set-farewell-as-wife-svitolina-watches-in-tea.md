The French crowd favorite battled over three hours on Philippe Chatrier, coming back from two sets down to force a decider before losing 6-0 in the fifth to Hugo Gaston. Monfils made the sign of the cross and tapped the clay in appreciation while his wife Elina Svitolina cried in the stands. The loss likely ends the 37-year-old's Roland Garros career in front of his home crowd.
