Osaka retired at the start of the second set in Saturday's Bad Homburg final after picking up a foot injury, handing her opponent the title. The timing is the worst possible: Wimbledon begins Monday, June 29, leaving her less than 48 hours to be assessed and cleared to compete. If she withdraws from the draw, her place passes to a lucky loser on the eve of the Slam.
