Elina Svitolina reached the Berlin Open Round of 16 after Anna Kalinskaya was forced to retire through injury, handing the Ukrainian a walkover into the last 16 of the WTA 500 grass event. Svitolina, who won the Rome title last week, now has back-to-back weeks of deep runs heading into the Wimbledon fortnight. The Berlin R16 appearance keeps her grass-court momentum alive at a WTA 500 just under three weeks before Wimbledon begins.
