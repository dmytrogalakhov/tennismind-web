The 2015 champion was honored with a tribute ceremony following his first-round loss to Jesper De Jong on Monday, marking his 21st and final appearance at Roland Garros. Tournament organizers presented him with a trophy and showed video messages from Federer, Nadal, Djokovic, Alcaraz, Sinner and other stars celebrating his career.
