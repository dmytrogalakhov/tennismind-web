Multiple players are cutting press conferences short in a coordinated action following discussions that began in Rome. Defending champion Coco Gauff confirmed her participation, saying players chose to limit TV and tournament partner obligations while still answering questions from journalists to avoid punishing media who 'have nothing to do with this.'
