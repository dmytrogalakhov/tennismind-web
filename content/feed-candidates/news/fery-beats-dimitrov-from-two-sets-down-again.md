Arthur Fery beat Grigor Dimitrov in five sets on Monday, coming back from a break down in the fourth set to force a fifth-set tiebreak — his second match in a row won from a seemingly losing position after he had also trailed Zizou Bergs 4-1 in both the fourth and fifth sets on Saturday. The British wildcard now faces ninth seed Flavio Cobolli in the quarterfinals, a player he has already beaten at the Australian Open. Fery has become the home crowd's focal point at Wimbledon, reaching the last eight without having held a lead at the decisive stage in either of his last two matches.
