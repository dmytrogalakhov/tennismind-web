Venus Williams, 45, is back at Roland Garros for the first time since 2021, partnering with 24-year-old Hailey Baptiste in women's doubles. The age gap of 21 years between the partners makes this one of the most intriguing pairings in recent Grand Slam history. Williams' return adds star power to the doubles draw as she continues her selective tournament schedule.
