Jack Draper will make his comeback from injury at Eastbourne, with three-time Grand Slam champion Andy Murray installed as his new coach. Emma Raducanu, meanwhile, has opted not to take a wildcard for the same event. Eastbourne is the final grass warm-up tournament before Wimbledon, making Draper's fitness and form there directly relevant to his preparation for the home Slam.
