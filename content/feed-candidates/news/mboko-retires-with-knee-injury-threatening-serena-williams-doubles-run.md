Victoria Mboko retired from her Queen's Club singles match after slipping on grass and injuring her left knee while trailing Karolina Pliskova 6-2, 3-4. The 19-year-old Canadian is partnering Serena Williams in doubles at the WTA 500 event, putting their comeback run in jeopardy. Mboko had been holding a break point that would have given her a chance to serve for the second set when she fell chasing down a Pliskova forehand.
