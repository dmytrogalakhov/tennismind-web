The 2015 French Open champion was honored with a tribute ceremony after losing his first-round match to Jesper De Jong on May 25, ending his 21-year Roland Garros career. Tournament organizers presented him with a trophy and showed video messages from Federer, Nadal, Djokovic, Alcaraz and Sinner celebrating his legacy at the tournament where he captured his most memorable Grand Slam title.
