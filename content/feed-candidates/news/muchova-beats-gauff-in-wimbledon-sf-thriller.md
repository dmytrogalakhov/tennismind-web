Karolina Muchova defeated Coco Gauff 6-2, 2-6, 7-6(10) in a rollercoaster semifinal that was decided by a super-tiebreaker. Gauff levelled after losing the first set, but Muchova held her nerve through the tension-packed decider to advance. She becomes the fourth Czech woman in the past six years to reach the Wimbledon final.
