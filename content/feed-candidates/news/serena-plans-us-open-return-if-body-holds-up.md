Rennae Stubbs, one of Serena Williams' coaches, confirmed at Wimbledon on Tuesday that Serena intends to continue her comeback and play the US Open if she is healthy. The announcement comes one week after Serena lost in her first singles match since 2022. The US Open, where Serena won six of her 23 Grand Slam singles titles, would be her next opportunity to extend a comeback that has only just resumed.
