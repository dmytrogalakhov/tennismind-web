2024 Paris Olympics gold medalist Qinwen Zheng lost 6-4, 6-0 to Poland's Maja Chwalinska, ranked 203rd in the world. Zheng was emotional in her post-match press conference, citing injury struggles that prevented proper clay court preparation. The upset adds to a historically depleted draw with 15 seeds already eliminated in round one.
