Alex De Minaur made no attempt to conceal his anguish after losing to Flavio Cobolli on Monday, telling reporters: 'It breaks me inside. That's the reality of it.' De Minaur had entered the draw with genuine title ambitions and the defeat ends what had been one of his deepest Wimbledon runs. Cobolli, who had already eliminated one other top-ten seed before De Minaur, moves into the quarterfinals as one of the tournament's most dangerous floaters.
