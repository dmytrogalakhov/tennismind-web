Serena Williams has confirmed she will partner Czech Karolina Muchova at next week's Berlin Open as she continues her return to professional tennis. The announcement comes as Williams builds toward what many expect to be a Wimbledon appearance. The partnership pairs the 23-time Grand Slam champion with the world No. 12, giving Williams a high-level doubles partner for her comeback preparation.
