Taylor Fritz lost 6-4, 6-4, 6-2 to Alexander Zverev on No. 1 Court, but the scoreline only tells part of the story: knee tendonitis flared during the match, limiting Fritz's movement and serve effectiveness throughout. Fritz had been building genuine grass-court momentum entering the quarterfinal, making the injury's timing particularly costly. With no Americans left in either singles draw, the question of Fritz's fitness heading into the second half of the season now becomes the pressing concern.
