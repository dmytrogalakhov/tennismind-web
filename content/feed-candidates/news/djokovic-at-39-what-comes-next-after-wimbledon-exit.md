Djokovic arrived at Wimbledon looking rejuvenated and came within two wins of a record 25th major, only to be dismantled 6-4, 6-4, 6-4 by Sinner in a semifinal where, by his own admission, he was 'half a step late on basically any shot.' Despite the defeat, Djokovic has indicated he aims to continue playing at 39. The loss means his bid for a 25th Grand Slam title — which would have broken his own record of 24 — goes on hold with no major on his calendar until the US Open in August.
