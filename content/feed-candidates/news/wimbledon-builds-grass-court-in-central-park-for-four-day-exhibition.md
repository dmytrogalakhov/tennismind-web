The All England Club will construct a temporary grass court at Central Park's Wollman Rink from June 26-29, maintained by Wimbledon's own groundstaff. The centerpiece is 'The Wimbledon Court Invitational' exhibition doubles match on June 26, followed by public play opportunities through a ballot system. The event includes traditional Wimbledon hospitality with strawberries and cream, marking the tournament's most ambitious overseas promotional effort.
