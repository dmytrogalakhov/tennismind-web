Seeded No. 7, Gauff beat Tamara Korpatsch 6-2, 6-1 in 54 minutes — her fastest-ever win at Wimbledon — to advance to the second round on Monday. The result snaps what ESPN described as a grass-court skid for the American, who had struggled on the surface in recent weeks including a loss in Berlin. With Wimbledon now underway, the dominant display puts Gauff on track to make a deep run from her seeded position in the draw.
