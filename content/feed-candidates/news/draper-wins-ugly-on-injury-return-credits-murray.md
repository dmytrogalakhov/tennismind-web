Jack Draper claimed victory in his first competitive match in over two months at the Eastbourne International, describing the win as "ugly" and crediting new coach Sir Andy Murray for inspiring the gritty approach. Draper said he drew directly on Murray's mentality to get through the difficult return from injury. The win puts Draper into the next round of a grass-court event with Wimbledon less than two weeks away, where he will be among the British hopes.
