Novak Djokovic was described as 'in full flow' in his second-round victory over Stefanos Tsitsipas, a performance that looked nothing like a 39-year-old who admitted to feeling less than fresh after his opener. Tsitsipas, now ranked No. 88, offered little resistance as Djokovic moved smoothly into the third round. With Alcaraz absent through injury and Sinner having looked shaky in round one, Djokovic is now on course for a potential semifinal rematch with Sinner — the same path that produced last year's final.
