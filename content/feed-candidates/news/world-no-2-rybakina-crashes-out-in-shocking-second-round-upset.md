Australian Open champion Elena Rybakina lost 3-6, 6-1, 7-6 (10-4) to world No. 55 Yuliia Starodubtseva, committing a staggering 71 unforced errors in what's being called the biggest upset of the tournament. With Rybakina gone and Alcaraz already withdrawn, Iga Swiatek becomes the heavy favorite in her half of the draw.
