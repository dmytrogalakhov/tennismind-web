Gaël Monfils battled for over three hours against Hugo Gaston, winning sets 3 and 4 after being down two sets to force a decider, but lost the fifth set 6-0. The 37-year-old French crowd favorite made the sign of the cross and tapped the Philippe Chatrier court in appreciation as the home crowd roared. His wife Elina Svitolina was seen crying in the stands as the emotional match ended.
