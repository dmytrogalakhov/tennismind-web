Alfie Hewett came from a set down to defeat Gustavo Fernandez and book his place in the Wimbledon wheelchair singles final for the fifth consecutive year. The British No. 1 showed the kind of resilience that has made him the dominant force in the wheelchair game on grass. Five straight finals at the same Grand Slam is a mark of extraordinary consistency at the sport's most prestigious wheelchair event.
