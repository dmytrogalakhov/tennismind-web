No. 9 seed Linda Noskova and No. 10 Karolina Muchova will contest Saturday's Wimbledon women's final in a matchup ESPN experts describe as a clash of two of the most effective serves in the game, with Noskova's having been 'almost untouchable as the tournament has progressed.' For Muchova, who battled through heat and physical pain to beat Gauff in the semis, it is a shot at her first Grand Slam title. It is the first time in nearly a decade that two women representing the same country have faced each other in a major final.
