Wimbledon bans players from using mobile phones during matches, but Alexander Zverev has been granted an exemption — a detail that caught viewers' attention during his semi-final run to the final. The BBC's Ask Me Anything team confirmed the exemption is in place, though the specific medical or logistical grounds were not detailed in the published explanation. With Zverev now in the Wimbledon final against Sinner, the policy is likely to draw further scrutiny on the sport's biggest stage.
