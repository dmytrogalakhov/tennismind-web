The 2015 French Open champion was honored on court after losing to Jesper De Jong in his 21st and final Roland Garros appearance. Tournament organizers presented him with a custom trophy and showed tribute videos featuring messages from Federer, Nadal, Djokovic and other stars. The 41-year-old finishes his career ranked ninth in all-time Roland Garros main draw wins with 46.
