Since 2023, the tournament's tattoo parlour has become the most requested amenity after hair salons, with one player getting 'Victory belongs to the most tenacious' inked as a Roland Garros memento. The player services also include an on-site seamstress, candy room, and a new Instagram-style app feature where Disneyland tickets have been the most popular offering this year.
