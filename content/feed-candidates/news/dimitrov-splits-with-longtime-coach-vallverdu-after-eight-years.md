Grigor Dimitrov has parted ways with head coach Daniel Vallverdu after an eight-year partnership, with both posting heartfelt messages on social media. The split comes amid a wave of high-profile coaching changes in men's tennis, including Carlos Alcaraz's recent separation from Juan Carlos Ferrero. Dimitrov, 34, thanked Vallverdu for 'believing in me and pushing me to be my best' as he enters the grass season without his longtime mentor.
