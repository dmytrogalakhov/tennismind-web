World No. 2 Carlos Alcaraz announced he's withdrawing from both Roland Garros (where he's the two-time defending champion) and Wimbledon due to a right wrist injury sustained at the Barcelona Open in April. This marks the first time Alcaraz has missed Wimbledon since turning pro in 2020, and creates a major opening for Jannik Sinner to further establish his dominance at the top of men's tennis.
