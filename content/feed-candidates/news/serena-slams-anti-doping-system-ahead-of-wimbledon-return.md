Serena Williams revealed that the rigours of anti-doping protocols almost convinced her not to return to professional tennis, describing the system as "unprofessional" and "unreasonable" in comments made ahead of her Wimbledon first-round match. She said the "gruelling" nature of the testing process was a serious obstacle she had to overcome before committing to the comeback. The remarks add a charged backdrop to her return at the All England Club, where she and Venus have already been handed a doubles wildcard.
