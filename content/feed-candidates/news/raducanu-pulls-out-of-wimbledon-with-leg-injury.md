Raducanu has withdrawn from Wimbledon with a leg injury, confirmed on the eve of the tournament's opening day. The withdrawal comes days after she reached the Bad Homburg final, suggesting the injury may have been sustained or aggravated during that run. For British fans, it removes the home crowd's most anticipated singles draw on the women's side before a ball has been struck.
