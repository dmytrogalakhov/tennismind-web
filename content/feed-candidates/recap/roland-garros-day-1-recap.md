MEN'S DRAW
No. 2 seed Alexander Zverev cruised past Benjamin Bonzi 6-3, 6-4, 6-2, while Novak Djokovic began his 25th Grand Slam quest with a four-set victory over Giovanni Mpetshi Perricard in the night session. The day's biggest upset saw No. 7 seed Taylor Fritz fall to American Nishesh Basavareddy in four sets. No. 28 seed Joao Fonseca defeated French qualifier Luka Pavlovic, and James Duckworth advanced when Gabriel Diallo retired at 6-3, 4-1.

WOMEN'S DRAW
No. 15 seed Marta Kostyuk swept past Oksana Selekhmeteva 6-2, 6-3 in her first match since her Madrid breakthrough. No. 8 seed Mirra Andreeva overcame French wild card Fiona Ferro in straight sets, while No. 26 seed Hailey Baptiste outlasted 2021 champion Barbora Krejcikova in a near three-hour thriller. No. 13 seed Karen Khachanov defeated French wild card Arthur Gea 6-3, 7-5, 6-4.

STAT OF THE DAY
This marks the 125th edition of Roland Garros and the 96th Grand Slam tournament, making it the oldest of the four majors still contested at its original venue since 1928.
