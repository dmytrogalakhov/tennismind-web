MEN'S DRAW
Novak Djokovic ground past French wildcard Victor Royer in four sets, denying the home crowd an upset on Philippe Chatrier. Brazilian teenager Joao Fonseca delivered the day's most thrilling match, coming back from two sets to one down to beat Dino Prizmic in a five-set epic. Spanish 18-year-old Rafael Jodar continued his breakout run with another convincing win, confirming his status as the tournament's rising star. French qualifier Quentin Halys upset No. 32 Ugo Humbert in straight sets, while Thiago Tirante stunned No. 21 Alejandro Davidovich Fokina after the Spaniard was reportedly abandoned mid-tournament by his coach.

WOMEN'S DRAW
The biggest shock saw Ukrainian qualifier Yuliia Starodubtseva defeat No. 2 Elena Rybakina in three sets, sending shockwaves through the draw. No. 1 Iga Swiatek cruised past Sara Bejlek in straight sets to reach the third round. Other seeded casualties included No. 13 Jasmine Paolini, who fell to Solana Sierra, and No. 29 Jelena Ostapenko, defeated by Magda Linette.
