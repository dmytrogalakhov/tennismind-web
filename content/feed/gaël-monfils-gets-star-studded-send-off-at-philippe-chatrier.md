French legend Gaël Monfils received a spectacular farewell ceremony with 15,000 fans at Court Philippe-Chatrier, featuring appearances by Sinner, Djokovic, Svitolina and Osaka in a night of tennis and entertainment. The 37-year-old faces Hugo Gaston in what will be his final Roland Garros first round. The emotional tribute caps a 19-year career for one of tennis's most beloved entertainers.
