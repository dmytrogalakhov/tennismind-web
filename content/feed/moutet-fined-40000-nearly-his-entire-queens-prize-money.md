The ATP has fined Corentin Moutet $40,000 for swearing seven times during a live BBC television interview at Queen's Club, a sum that wipes out almost all of his prize money from the tournament. The fine was confirmed Friday, a day after the incident aired and drew immediate attention from broadcasters and the tour. With Wimbledon two weeks away, Moutet heads to SW19 having effectively played Queen's for nothing.
