Gauff rallied past compatriot Jessica Pegula on Tuesday to reach her first Wimbledon semifinal at age 22. The ESPN source notes she is the youngest player since 2007 to have reached the semifinal stage at all four Grand Slams. She now awaits her semifinal opponent at the All England Club, having never previously gone this deep at Wimbledon.
