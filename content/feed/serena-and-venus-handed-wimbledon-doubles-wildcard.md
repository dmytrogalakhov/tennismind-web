Wimbledon has awarded Venus and Serena Williams a wildcard to compete in the women's doubles, reuniting the most decorated doubles partnership in the Open Era on the sport's biggest grass stage. The last time they played together was the 2022 US Open, where they lost in the opening round. Wimbledon begins in under two weeks, meaning this is one of the final confirmed entries before the draw is made.
