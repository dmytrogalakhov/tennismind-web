The American has climbed to No. 5 in the latest ATP rankings, moving ahead of Felix Auger-Aliassime who drops to sixth. At 23, Shelton becomes the youngest American man in the top 5 since Andy Roddick in 2004. His rise puts him in position to potentially overtake Taylor Fritz as the top-ranked American player.
