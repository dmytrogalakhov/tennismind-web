Alexander Zverev needed to dig deep to dispatch Jiří Kopřiva on the Halle grass, but the result delivered a historic footnote: he now equals Rafael Nadal's all-time ATP 500 win tally. The defending Roland Garros champion is building momentum on a surface where he has won Halle multiple times. With Wimbledon three weeks away, every match on grass counts toward form and seeding.
