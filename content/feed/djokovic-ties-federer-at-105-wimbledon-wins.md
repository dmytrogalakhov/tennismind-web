Djokovic defeated Arthur Rinderknech on Centre Court to record his 105th singles match win at Wimbledon, drawing level with Roger Federer for the most in men's history. The milestone arrived in the third round, meaning Djokovic needs only to keep winning to stand alone at the top of that record. With the tournament still in its early stages, every match from here is a chance to break the tie outright.
