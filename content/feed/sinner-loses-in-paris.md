Jannik Sinner is out of Roland Garros after a shock second-round loss to Juan Manuel Cerundolo, who came back from two sets down to win 3-6, 2-6, 7-5, 6-1, 6-1. The result blows open the men’s draw and turns the tournament from a Sinner-led campaign into a wide-open title race.
