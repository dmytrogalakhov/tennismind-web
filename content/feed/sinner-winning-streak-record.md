Jannik Sinner’s ATP Masters 1000 winning streak has reached 34 consecutive match wins, surpassing Novak Djokovic’s previous record of 31. That makes the run both a new benchmark for Masters consistency and one of the clearest signs of how dominant Sinner has been through the 2026 spring swing.
