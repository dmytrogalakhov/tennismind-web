🏆 Alexander Zverev is a Grand Slam champion. The German finally broke through in his fourth major final, beating Flavio Cobolli 6-1, 4-6, 6-4, 6-7(5), 6-1 to win Roland Garros — ending years of near-misses and heartbreak at the biggest moments. With Sinner out early and Alcaraz absent, the world No. 3 seized the opening and refused to let a spirited Cobolli fightback, which forced a fifth set, deny him. For Cobolli, defeat still brings a maiden Grand Slam final, a Top 10 debut on Monday, and the heartbreak of falling one set short of becoming the first Italian man to win in Paris since Panatta in 1976.
