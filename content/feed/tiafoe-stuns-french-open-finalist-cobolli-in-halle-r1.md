Frances Tiafoe dispatched French Open finalist Flavio Cobolli 6-2, 7-6(4) in the first round of the Terra Wortmann Open in Halle. The result marked Tiafoe's first top-10 win since 2024, a sharp reminder that the American can be dangerous on the grass-court swing. With Wimbledon less than three weeks away, a run in Halle would be a timely confidence boost for Tiafoe.
