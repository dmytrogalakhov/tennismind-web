Serena Williams will return to singles competition at Wimbledon, BBC Sport confirmed Monday, with an analysis of her readiness and the physical challenges she faces at age 44. She has already been handed a doubles wildcard alongside Venus for the tournament. Wimbledon is roughly ten days away, meaning any fitness questions will be answered almost immediately on the sport's biggest grass stage.
