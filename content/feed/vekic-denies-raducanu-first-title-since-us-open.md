Emma Raducanu's wait for a first title since her 2021 US Open triumph continues after falling to Croatian lucky loser Donna Vekic in the Queen's Club final. Vekic dominated the opening set 6-0 before holding off Raducanu's comeback attempt in a second-set tiebreak. The defeat means Raducanu remains without a WTA title since her stunning Grand Slam breakthrough nearly five years ago.
