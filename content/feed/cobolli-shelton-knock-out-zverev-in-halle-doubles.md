Flavio Cobolli and Ben Shelton combined to defeat Alexander Zverev and his partner Melo in the Halle Open doubles, knocking out the reigning singles finalist on his home ATP 500 grass court. The pairing of two players who met in singles earlier this week adds an unusual dynamic to the result. With Wimbledon three weeks away, both Cobolli and Shelton pick up valuable grass-court match time at an ATP 500 event.
