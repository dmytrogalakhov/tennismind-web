But Medvedev already beat Cilic convincingly on grass just two weeks ago at 's-Hertogenbosch, and his ability to neutralize big servers with his return game is precisely the weapon that makes Cilic's serve-heavy game plan harder to execute than it looks. Medvedev in four, tidier than their last meeting but not without a fight.
