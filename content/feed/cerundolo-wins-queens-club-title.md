Francisco Cerundolo came from a first-set tiebreak loss to defeat Tommy Paul 6-7(4), 6-4, 6-3 in the Queen's Club final on Sunday. The Argentine won the last two sets convincingly after dropping the opener, denying Paul a second Queen's title. Wimbledon begins in ten days, and Cerundolo heads there as the reigning champion of the sport's most prestigious grass warm-up event.
