French teenager Moïse Kouamé became the youngest man to reach the third round of a Grand Slam since Rafael Nadal at Wimbledon in 2003. The 17-year-old from Paris needed nearly five hours to get there, beating former US Open champion Marin Čilić in a marathon. When the last player your name sits next to in the record books is Nadal, you're doing something right.
