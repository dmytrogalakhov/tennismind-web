Marie Bouzkova defeated Emma Navarro to win the Lexus Nottingham Open, a WTA 250 event on grass. Navarro, who had reached her second final of the 2026 season, could not convert the occasion into a title. With Wimbledon beginning in under two weeks, Bouzkova heads to SW19 with a grass-court title in hand while Navarro takes ranking points from a finalist run.
