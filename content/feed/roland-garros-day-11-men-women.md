MEN'S DRAW

Flavio Cobolli completed a stunning comeback from a set down to defeat Félix Auger-Aliassime 4-6, 6-4, 6-4, 6-4, reaching his first Grand Slam semifinal. After losing the opener, the Italian found his serve and won 86 percent of his first-serve points in the second set to turn the match around, despite Auger-Aliassime earning seven break points in the third set. Matteo Arnaldi defeated compatriot Matteo Berrettini 7-5, 5-2 when the former Wimbledon finalist retired due to injury, guaranteeing Italy at least one men's semifinalist.

WOMEN'S DRAW

The tournament lost its top seed as Diana Shnaider stunned world No. 1 Aryna Sabalenka 3-6, 7-5, 6-0 in a remarkable comeback, winning the final 10 games after being two points from defeat with Sabalenka serving at 5-4 in the second set. The Russian's victory blows the draw wide open and sends her to a first Grand Slam semifinal. Polish qualifier Maja Chwalińska continued her extraordinary run by defeating Anna Kalinskaya 7-6, 6-3, becoming only the second Polish woman to reach the Roland Garros semifinals alongside four-time champion Iga Swiatek.
