**MEN'S DRAW**

Matteo Berrettini's resurgent clay season continued with a commanding straight-sets victory over Juan Manuel Cerúndolo, positioning the former Wimbledon finalist as a dangerous floater in the fourth round. Félix Auger-Aliassime swept past Alejandro Tabilo in straight sets to reach his first Roland Garros round of 16 since 2022. Matteo Arnaldi stunned Frances Tiafoe in a grueling five-setter, while Flavio Cobolli survived a scare against American Zachary Svajda, dropping the third set in a tiebreak before closing out the fourth-set tiebreaker.

**WOMEN'S DRAW**

Aryna Sabalenka delivered a masterclass against four-time Grand Slam champion Naomi Osaka, winning 7-5, 6-3 to reach the fourth round. The world No. 2 controlled the net exchanges and capitalized on her second chances after Osaka's early break, asserting her dominance as the tournament's top remaining seed. Polish qualifier Maja Chwalińska continued her stunning run by defeating home favorite Diane Parry in straight sets, while Diana Shnaider rallied from a set down to eliminate Madison Keys. Anna Kalinskaya survived a three-set thriller against Anastasia Potapova.
