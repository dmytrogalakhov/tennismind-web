MEN'S DRAW
Cobolli keeps dismantling seeds, a British wildcard produced the match of the tournament, and Fritz moves quietly into the quarterfinals.

• Cobolli🇮🇹 def. de Minaur🇦🇺 in straight sets — the Italian has now eliminated two top-ten seeds in succession, and de Minaur, who expressed raw devastation afterwards, exits a draw he had genuine title ambitions in; Cobolli is through to the quarterfinals and cannot be ignored
• Fery🇬🇧 def. Dimitrov🇧🇬 in five — the British wildcard came back from two sets to one down to beat the Bulgarian in a fifth-set tiebreak, winning two consecutive matches from seemingly hopeless positions; the home crowd has its hero, and Fery is in the quarterfinals on a wildcard
• Fritz🇺🇸 def. Bublik🇰🇿 in straight sets — efficient and controlled, the American moves through without drama into the quarterfinals

WOMEN'S DRAW
Kostyuk reaches her first Wimbledon quarterfinal, Paolini survives a scare, and the draw's remaining field is taking sharp shape.

• Kostyuk🇺🇦 def. Krueger🇺🇸 in straight sets — a first Wimbledon quarterfinal for the Ukrainian, who has now won the Madrid title, reached the French Open semifinals, and is deep at Wimbledon all in the same season; a career-best run with real momentum behind it
• Paolini🇮🇹 def. Eala🇵🇭 in three — the Italian dropped the second set before closing out, ending the Filipino's breakthrough run; the defending finalist is through and finding her footing at the right moment
• Noskova🇨🇿 def. Keys🇺🇸 in straight sets — Keys, who had been building genuine title momentum, is eliminated; Noskova advances into the quarterfinals of a draw that has shed its biggest names
• Mertens🇧🇪 def. Bouzkova🇨🇿 in straight sets — the Belgian moves through efficiently, continuing a run that began with the elimination of the second seed earlier in the fortnight
