Mirra Andreeva, who won Roland Garros just weeks ago, was eliminated by Barbora Krejcikova at Wimbledon and broke down crying in her post-match news conference, describing the defeat as an emotional blow. Krejcikova, a former Wimbledon champion, proved that grass-court pedigree still counts even against the hottest player on tour. Andreeva's early exit means the French Open title has not translated into a deep run at the All England Club, leaving the women's draw more open at the top.
