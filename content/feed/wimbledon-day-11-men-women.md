WOMEN'S DRAW
Wimbledon's women's final is an all-Czech affair — and it was built on drama, resilience, and a missed match point.

• Muchova🇨🇿 def. Gauff🇺🇸 in three sets — Gauff served for the match and held a match point before Muchova, battling heat and physical pain, clawed back to win a 7-6 deciding set; the American, who had been among the draw's genuine title favourites after Sabalenka and Krejcikova fell, exits without the major she has been chasing, while Muchova reaches her second Grand Slam final
• Noskova🇨🇿 def. Kostyuk🇺🇦 in straight sets — a clinical 6-4, 6-4 dismantling of a Ukrainian player whose season had already produced Madrid, a French Open semifinal, and a first Wimbledon semifinal; Noskova, who has not dropped a set across either semifinal match, arrives at Saturday's final as the fresher player and the form story of the fortnight
