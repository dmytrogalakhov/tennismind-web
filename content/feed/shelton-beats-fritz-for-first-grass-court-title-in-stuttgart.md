Ben Shelton defeated defending champion Taylor Fritz to win the Stuttgart Open, capturing his first grass-court title in an all-American final. The victory adds to Shelton's breakthrough season and gives him crucial confidence heading into Wimbledon. Fritz was unable to defend his 2025 Stuttgart crown, falling to his compatriot in straight sets.
