Mensik's powerful serve and five-set endurance meets Fonseca's explosive forehand and momentum from stunning wins over Djokovic and Ruud. Fonseca can control this match if his forehand stays hot and he constructs points patiently on clay, especially after showing he can handle pressure in long battles. But Mensik's serve gives him more free points, and his proven ability to recover from deficits in deciding sets makes him dangerous when matches stretch deep. Mensik in five if he weathers the early storm.
