Elina Svitolina defeated Coco Gauff 6-4, 6-7(3), 6-2 to win the Italian Open for the third time. The Ukrainian beat the world Nos. 2, 3 and 4 — Rybakina, Swiatek and Gauff — in her last three matches. She now heads to Roland Garros on the back of her first WTA 1000 title since Rome 2018.
