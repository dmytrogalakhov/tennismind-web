Frances Tiafoe swept past fifth-seeded Taylor Fritz 6-4, 6-4 in a clean, dominant all-American final at the Halle Open. Fritz had beaten French Open champion Alexander Zverev to reach the final, making Tiafoe's straight-sets victory all the more emphatic. With Wimbledon beginning in ten days, Tiafoe takes the title at one of the premier ATP 500 grass-court events on the calendar.
