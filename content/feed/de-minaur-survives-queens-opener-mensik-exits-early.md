Alex de Minaur described his first-round win at Queen's Club as being "all about surviving," signalling a scrappy start to his grass campaign at the ATP 500 event. Jakub Mensik, meanwhile, suffered an early exit, thinning the top of the draw with Wimbledon now less than two weeks away. For de Minaur, who was upset in Halle last week by Majchrzak, advancing at Queen's is the last meaningful grass-court opportunity before Wimbledon seedings are finalised.
