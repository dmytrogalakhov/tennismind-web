MEN'S DRAW
A British wildcard nobody seeded rewrote the quarterfinal script, and Zverev reminded the draw who its favourite is.

• Fery🇬🇧 def. Cobolli🇮🇹 in straight sets — the unseeded British wildcard, who has won consecutive five-set matches from losing positions to reach this stage, now eliminates the Italian who had dismantled two top-ten seeds; Fery becomes the first British wildcard, man or woman, to reach a Slam semifinal in the Open Era, and the bottom half of the draw has lost its most dangerous floater
• Zverev🇩🇪 def. Fritz🇺🇸 in straight sets — a 6-4, 6-4, 6-2 demolition of a player who had been building genuine grass-court momentum; Fritz barely troubled the German, and Zverev moves into the semifinals as the section's commanding favourite

WOMEN'S DRAW
Kostyuk's Centre Court debut produced a statement; Noskova confirmed her quarterfinal form was no accident.

• Kostyuk🇺🇦 def. Paolini🇮🇹 in straight sets — a 6-3, 6-2 dismantling of the defending finalist on her Centre Court debut; Paolini, who had been finding her footing at exactly the right moment, was given no foothold at all, and Kostyuk's semifinal run now stands as one of the season's most compelling stories
• Noskova🇨🇿 def. Mertens🇧🇪 in straight sets — Mertens, whose run included the elimination of the second seed, is stopped efficiently; Noskova advances to the semifinals having dropped no sets in either quarterfinal match
