Serena Williams and Karolina Muchova were knocked out in the first round of the Berlin Open doubles, losing 6-4, 6-4 to the team of Giuliana Olmos and Erin Routliffe. The defeat comes just days after Serena and Venus were handed a Wimbledon doubles wildcard, meaning Serena's grass-court match sharpness ahead of the All England Club will now rest entirely on that tournament.
