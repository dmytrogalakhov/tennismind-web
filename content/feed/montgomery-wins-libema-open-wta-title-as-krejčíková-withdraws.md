Robin Montgomery claimed the Libema Open WTA title in 's-Hertogenbosch when Barbora Krejčíková was forced to withdraw from the final, giving the American a walkover victory. Krejčíková had beaten Magda Linette 6–3, 7–6 in the semifinals to reach Sunday's final before pulling out without taking to the court. Montgomery leaves the Netherlands with a grass court title three weeks before Wimbledon.
