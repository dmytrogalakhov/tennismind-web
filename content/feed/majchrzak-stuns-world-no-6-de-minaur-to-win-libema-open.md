Unseeded Kamil Majchrzak, ranked 76 in the world, defeated second seed Alex de Minaur 6–3, 2–6, 7–6(5) to win the Libema Open title on grass in 's-Hertogenbosch. The Polish player had already beaten higher-ranked opponents on his way to the final, where he outlasted the world No. 6 in a deciding tiebreak — a 70-place ranking gap between winner and runner-up. De Minaur heads to Wimbledon without a grass title this season after falling to an unseeded opponent in three sets.
