In tennis slang, a 6-0 set is a bagel and a 6-1 set is a breadstick — which is why the internet started calling Iga Świątek’s dominant scorelines “Iga’s Bakery.” The meme is funny because it fits her ruthless clay-court dominance, but Iga herself has said she does not like leaning into it because she wants to stay respectful to her opponents.
