MEN'S DRAW
Flavio Cobolli cruised past Learner Tien in straight sets, and Alejandro Tabilo had to battle through Moïse Kouamé in a tighter four-setter. Matteo Berrettini outlasted Francisco Comesaña in a day-session marathon, while Juan Manuel Cerúndolo survived Martín Landaluce in another long four-set fight. Matteo Arnaldi edged Raphaël Collignon, Frances Tiafoe came from behind to beat Jaime Faria, and Félix Auger-Aliassime closed out Brandon Nakashima in four.

WOMEN'S DRAW
Anastasia Potapova stunned Coco Gauff in three sets, Madison Keys outlasted Victoria Mboko in a marathon three-set battle, and Naomi Osaka survived Iva Jovic. Aryna Sabalenka cruised past Daria Kasatkina, Diane Parry edged Amanda Anisimova in a thriller, while Diana Shnaider, Maja Chwalińska and Anna Kalinskaya all moved through.
