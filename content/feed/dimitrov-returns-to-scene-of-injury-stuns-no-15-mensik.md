Nearly one year to the day after sustaining a torn pectoral muscle at Wimbledon against Jannik Sinner, Grigor Dimitrov has beaten 15th seed Jakub Mensik to advance at this year's Championships. The Bulgarian's return to the court where his injury occurred — and his win over a seeded opponent — gives the comeback a specific, measurable weight beyond mere participation. Mensik, seeded 15th, is the highest-ranked player Dimitrov has beaten at a Grand Slam since his injury, making the result a concrete marker of his recovery.
