**MEN'S DRAW**

Brazilian teenager João Fonseca delivered the day's biggest statement, stunning No. 15 seed Casper Ruud in four sets to reach his first Grand Slam fourth round. Spanish qualifier Rafael Jodar completed an extraordinary comeback from two sets down to defeat former world No. 10 Pablo Carreño Busta in five sets, while 18-year-old Jakub Mensik upset No. 6 seed Andrey Rublev in another five-set thriller. No. 2 seed Alexander Zverev advanced comfortably past Jesper de Jong in straight sets.

**WOMEN'S DRAW**

The tournament lost its biggest star as No. 3 seed Iga Swiatek crashed out to Marta Kostyuk in straight sets, sending shockwaves through Roland Garros. The Ukrainian's 7-5, 6-1 victory blows the draw wide open and ends Swiatek's bid for a fourth Paris title. Elina Svitolina rallied from a set down to defeat Belinda Bencic, while 17-year-old Mirra Andreeva cruised past Jil Teichmann. Sorana Cirstea, playing her final Roland Garros before retirement, advanced with a straight-sets win over Wang Xiyu.
