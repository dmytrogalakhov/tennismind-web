Taylor Fritz has beaten Alexander Zverev to reach the final of the Halle Open, an ATP 500 on grass. Zverev, who equalled Nadal's ATP 500 win record earlier this week, is eliminated at the semifinal stage. With Wimbledon less than two weeks away, Fritz now has a Halle final on grass as his last competitive match before the Slam.
