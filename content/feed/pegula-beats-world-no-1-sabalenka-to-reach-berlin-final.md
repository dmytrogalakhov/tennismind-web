Jessica Pegula defeated world number one Aryna Sabalenka in three sets, with Sabalenka failing to win a single game in the deciding set — a 6-0 collapse that mirrors a pattern the BBC notes has now happened to her in multiple deciding sets this week. Pegula advances to the Berlin Open final on grass, two weeks before Wimbledon begins. The result means Sabalenka, the top seed, exits without a title at a WTA 500 grass event that serves as one of the last major Wimbledon tune-ups on the calendar.
