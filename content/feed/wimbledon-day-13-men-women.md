WOMEN'S DRAW
Noskova clawed back from the brink of collapse — five championship points squandered, five consecutive games lost — to become Wimbledon champion in one of the most dramatic finals the All England Club has seen.

• Noskova🇨🇿 def. Muchova🇨🇿 in three sets — leading 6-2, 5-2 and holding five championship points, Noskova watched her advantage disintegrate as Muchova stormed back to force a third set; what followed required immense resilience from the ninth seed, who ultimately leaned on her dominance on serve and return to close out 6-3 and lift the Venus Rosewater Dish; an all-Czech final that ended with a first Grand Slam title for Noskova, and a second major final heartbreak for Muchova
