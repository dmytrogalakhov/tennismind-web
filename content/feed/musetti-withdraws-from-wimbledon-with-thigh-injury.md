Lorenzo Musetti has withdrawn from Wimbledon due to a left thigh injury, with Matteo Berrettini expected to take his place in the draw. The Italian had been one of the more anticipated grass-court contenders following his clay-court form this spring. His absence opens a spot in the draw for Berrettini, who returns to the venue where he reached the 2021 final.
