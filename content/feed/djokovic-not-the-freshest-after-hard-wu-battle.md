Novak Djokovic opened his Wimbledon campaign with a hard-fought win over China's Wu Yibing, admitting afterwards he was 'happy but not the freshest' after the contest. The victory keeps alive his bid for a record 25th Grand Slam title, but the laboured nature of the win — described by the BBC as a 'battle' — will raise questions about his sharpness on grass. Djokovic is chasing a standalone record 25th major, and a difficult opener against a player ranked well outside the top 10 is not the ideal signal for the fortnight ahead.
