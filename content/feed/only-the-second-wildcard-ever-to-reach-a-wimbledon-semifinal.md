Arthur Fery has beaten Flavio Cobolli to reach the Wimbledon semifinal — and in doing so has joined a list of exactly one. The only other wildcard to reach the last four at Wimbledon was Goran Ivanisevic, who did it in 2001 and won the title. Fery arrived at the tournament without a seeding, without a ranking to justify his place in the draw, and has dismantled every opponent who has stood in front of him on grass. He is 23 years old. He is in the semifinal of Wimbledon. Some things you just have to accept.
