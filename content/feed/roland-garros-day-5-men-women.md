MEN'S DRAW
The tournament was rocked by its biggest upset as World No. 1 Jannik Sinner crashed out to Argentine Juan Manuel Cerúndolo in five sets after leading two sets to love. The oppressive Paris heat proved decisive as Sinner visibly cramped and fatigued, allowing Cerúndolo to storm back for a stunning victory. French teenager Moïse Kouamé continued his breakthrough run, surviving a five-set thriller against Adolfo Daniel Vallejo. No. 10 Flavio Cobolli advanced in straight sets over Wu Yibing, while No. 18 Learner Tien won an epic five-setter against Facundo Diaz Acosta.

WOMEN'S DRAW
Polish qualifier Maja Chwalinska delivered another major upset, demolishing No. 23 Elise Mertens 6-4, 6-0 to reach the third round. Rising American star No. 17 Iva Jović cruised past Emma Navarro in straight sets to set up a blockbuster third-round clash with four-time Grand Slam champion Naomi Osaka. No. 16 Osaka advanced with a hard-fought victory over Donna Bekic, while No. 25 Diana Shnaider defeated McCartney Kessler in straight sets.
