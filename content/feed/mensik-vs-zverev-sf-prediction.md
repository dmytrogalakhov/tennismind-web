Mensik's fearless power hitting meets Zverev's tactical control and Grand Slam experience in a clash between the tournament's most confident players. Mensik can win if he replicates his straight-sets quarterfinal form and uses his massive serve to dictate early, especially since he's already taken a set off Zverev on clay this year in Madrid. But Zverev has dropped just one set all tournament, looks supremely composed under pressure, and knows how to absorb momentum swings in best-of-five tennis when chasing his first major title. Zverev in four sets.
