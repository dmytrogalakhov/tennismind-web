Novak Djokovic has publicly called on people to ease their criticism of Serena Williams following her singles loss in her return to competitive play at Wimbledon — her first singles match in nearly four years. The defence comes as Serena's agent also confirmed she tweaked her knee during the match, leaving her doubles participation with Venus in doubt. Djokovic's intervention puts one of the sport's biggest names squarely behind Serena at a moment when her fitness and competitive future are both under scrutiny.
