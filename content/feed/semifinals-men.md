MEN'S DRAW
Alexander Zverev is through to the Roland Garros final, outlasting Jakub Mensik in four sets, 7-5, 6-2, 3-6, 6-3. The young Czech took the third to drag Zverev into a fourth, but the German steadied and closed it out — reaching the final without dropping the level that has carried him through the draw. Flavio Cobolli advances in the cruelest way: his semifinal against fellow Italian Matteo Arnaldi never happened, with Arnaldi forced to withdraw through illness. Cobolli reaches the biggest final of his life on a walkover, denied the match but not the moment.
