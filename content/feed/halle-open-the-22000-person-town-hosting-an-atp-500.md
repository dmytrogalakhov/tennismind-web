Halle (Westfalen), with just 22,000 residents, hosts one of tennis's premier grass court events — the Terra Wortmann Open ATP 500. The tournament is so successful that hotels in the much larger city of Halle (Saale) regularly receive confused tennis fans who book the wrong location. This makes it one of the smallest host cities for any ATP 500 event, showcasing how local business vision can create world-class tennis in unexpected places.
