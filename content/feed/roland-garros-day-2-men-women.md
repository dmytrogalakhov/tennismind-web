MEN'S DRAW
Stan Wawrinka's legendary career at Roland Garros came to an emotional end as the 2015 champion fell to Dutchman Jesper de Jong 6-3, 3-6, 6-3, 6-4 in his final appearance at the tournament. The 41-year-old Swiss received a standing ovation from the Paris crowd in what was his 21st and last French Open. Fellow farewell warrior Gael Monfils also played his final Roland Garros match, falling to Hugo Gaston in front of an emotional home crowd. The 39-year-old Frenchman, who entertained Paris for two decades, received a hero's send-off on Philippe Chatrier. In the day's biggest upset, Pablo Carreno Busta stunned No. 12 seed Jiri Lehecka 6-3, 7-6(3), 6-3. No. 8 Alex de Minaur advanced comfortably past British qualifier Toby Samuel 6-4, 6-4, 6-2, while No. 24 Tommy Paul defeated Rinky Hijikata 4-6, 6-3, 7-5, 6-4.

WOMEN'S DRAW
No. 1 Iga Swiatek began her title defense with a commanding 6-1, 6-2 victory over 17-year-old Australian wild card Emerson Jones on Court Philippe-Chatrier. The biggest upset saw Polish qualifier Maja Chwalinska stun Olympic champion Qinwen Zheng 6-4, 6-0. No. 10 Karolina Muchova advanced with a 7-5, 6-2 win over Anastasia Zakharova, while American Alycia Parks defeated No. 24 Leylah Fernandez 6-4, 6-4.
