Hours after advancing to her first Wimbledon semifinal, Marta Kostyuk used her platform to slam the IOC's decision to provisionally lift its ban on Russia and recommend individual sports drop the neutral-athlete requirement. Kostyuk, one of the tour's most outspoken voices on the war in Ukraine, has consistently refused to shake hands with Russian and Belarusian opponents. Her semifinal appearance — the deepest Slam run of her career — gives her remarks unusual weight at one of the sport's most-watched stages.
