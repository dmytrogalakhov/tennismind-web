Jannik Sinner claimed his 100th Grand Slam match win at Wimbledon 2026, with coach Darren Cahill praising the world No. 1's 'amazing resilience' in getting the job done on grass. The milestone came despite Sinner already having navigated a shaky opener that left him with a bloody foot earlier in the fortnight. Reaching 100 Grand Slam wins places Sinner in elite historical company and underlines his status as the dominant force heading into the business end of the draw.
