Paula Badosa defeated Coco Gauff in three sets at the Berlin Open, with Badosa saying she 'finally saw herself on court' again after her well-documented injury struggles. The win over the world No. 2 at a WTA 500 on grass is the kind of result that resets expectations. With Wimbledon less than three weeks away, Badosa has now beaten one of the tournament's leading contenders on the exact surface that matters most.
