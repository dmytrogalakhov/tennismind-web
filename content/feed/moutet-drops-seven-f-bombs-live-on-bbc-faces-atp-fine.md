Corentin Moutet turned a post-match BBC interview at Queen's Club into a profanity-laden spectacle, swearing seven times on live television before signing off with 'I hope you guys didn't get offended.' The ATP is now expected to issue a significant fine for the on-air outburst. The incident puts Moutet's off-court conduct under scrutiny at one of the sport's most traditional grass-court venues, two weeks before Wimbledon.
