Marketa Vondrousova has been suspended from tennis for four years after refusing an anti-doping test in December 2025, the sport's anti-doping authorities confirmed Monday. The ban is drawing controversy given Vondrousova's status as a former Grand Slam champion. The suspension rules her out of Wimbledon, which begins in under two weeks, and effectively sidelines her through 2029.
