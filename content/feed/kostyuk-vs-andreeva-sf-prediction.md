Kostyuk's relentless clay court power meets Andreeva's tactical precision in a matchup where the Ukrainian's 17-match clay winning streak faces the Russian's Grand Slam experience. Andreeva can disrupt Kostyuk's rhythm if she varies pace and uses her superior court craft to pull the Ukrainian out of position, especially with her improved net play and drop shot. But Kostyuk's forehand is technically superior and her Madrid final victory over Andreeva just one month ago gives her both the tactical blueprint and psychological edge in the biggest moments. Kostyuk in three sets to reach her first Grand Slam final.
