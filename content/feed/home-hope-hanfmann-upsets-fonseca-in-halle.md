Yannick Hanfmann delivered a crowd-pleasing upset at the Halle Open, defeating João Fonseca on the German grass to advance in the ATP 500 event. Playing in front of a supportive home crowd, Hanfmann capitalised on the grass-court conditions that suit his serve-and-volley game. The result is a blow for Fonseca, one of the tour's most hyped young talents, as both players look to sharpen their games ahead of Wimbledon.
