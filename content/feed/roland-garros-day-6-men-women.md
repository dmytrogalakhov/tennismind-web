**MEN'S DRAW**

No. 3 seed Alexander Zverev navigated a brief wobble against France's Quentin Halys, dropping the third set before closing out a four-set victory to reach the last 16. No. 11 seed Andrey Rublev stayed on course with a straight-sets win over Portugal's Nuno Borges, though he needed to win two tiebreaks to seal the deal.

**WOMEN'S DRAW**

The day's biggest shock came from Switzerland's Jil Teichmann, ranked 170th in the world, who stunned No. 10 seed Karolína Muchová in straight sets to reach the fourth round. Four-time Roland Garros champion Iga Świątek cruised past compatriot Magda Linette in an all-Polish encounter, while No. 7 seed Elina Svitolina dominated Germany's Tamara Korpatsch. No. 15 seed Marta Kostyuk extended her perfect 2026 clay record to 15-0 with a comfortable victory over Switzerland's Viktorija Golubic.
