A High Court ruling approved Wimbledon's plan to build 39 new grass courts, including an 8,000-seat show court on the Wimbledon Park Golf Course site. The All England Club has long been constrained by its footprint, with court availability limiting qualifying and practice capacity at the world's most prestigious grass-court venue. Adding 39 courts to a site that currently hosts the sport's only grass Grand Slam would represent the largest single expansion in the tournament's 139-year history.
