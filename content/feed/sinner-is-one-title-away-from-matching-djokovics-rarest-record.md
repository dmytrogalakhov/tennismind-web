If Jannik Sinner wins the Italian Open this week, he will complete the Career Golden Masters — winning all nine ATP Masters 1000 tournaments at least once. Only one player in history has achieved this: Novak Djokovic. At 24, Sinner would become the youngest to do it, adding Rome to a collection that already includes Indian Wells, Miami, Monte Carlo, Madrid, Canada, Cincinnati, Shanghai, and Paris. Doing it on home soil would make it even more historic.
