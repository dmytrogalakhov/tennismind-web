**MEN'S DRAW**

Alexander Zverev cruised past Spanish qualifier Rafael Jodar in straight sets 7-6, 6-1, 6-3 to reach his third consecutive Roland Garros semifinal. The German's dominant performance after a tight opening set positions him as the clear favorite in a wide-open bottom half. Jakub Mensik completed a stunning tournament run by defeating Brazilian teenager João Fonseca 6-4, 6-3, 7-6, setting up a semifinal clash with Zverev. The 20-year-old Czech's calm proved decisive in his Philippe Chatrier debut, though he needed seven match points to close out the victory after trailing 5-3 in the third set.

**WOMEN'S DRAW**

Mirra Andreeva demolished Sorana Cirstea 6-0, 6-3 in what proved to be the Romanian veteran's final Roland Garros match before retirement. The 17-year-old Russian's ruthless performance sends her to the semifinals and confirms her status as the most dangerous unseeded player remaining. Marta Kostyuk survived an all-Ukrainian battle against Elina Svitolina 6-3, 2-6, 6-2, advancing to her first Grand Slam semifinal. The victory sets up a compelling clash between two of the tournament's breakthrough stars.
